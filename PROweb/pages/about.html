About TechPro Institute

15+ years of empowering careers through quality computer education

Our Story

Empowering India Through Digital Education Since 2010

TechPro Institute of Computer Education was founded in 2010 with a simple yet powerful vision: to make quality computer education accessible to every Indian, regardless of location or economic background.

Starting from a single center in Noida, we have grown to become one of India's most trusted computer education networks with 320+ franchise centers across 24 states, serving over 45,000 students every year.

🎯

Our Mission

To deliver industry-relevant computer education that creates skilled professionals capable of thriving in the digital economy.

🔭

Our Vision

To be India's #1 computer education network, touching 1 million lives through skill development by 2030.

Our Journey

15 Years of Growth

2010
Founded in Noida
TechPro Institute started with a single center and 3 courses, serving 50 students.
2013
Franchise Model Launch
Launched franchise program. Reached 25 centers across Uttar Pradesh within the first year.
2015
ISO Certification
Achieved ISO 9001:2015 certification. Expanded to 100+ centers and crossed 10,000 students.
2018
National Expansion
Expanded to 15+ states. Launched digital learning platform. 200+ franchise centers operational.
2020
NIELIT Recognition
Partnered with NIELIT for CCC and other government-recognized certificate programs.
2024
45,000+ Students
Currently serving 45,000+ students through 320+ centers across 24 states with 48+ courses.
